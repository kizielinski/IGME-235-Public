Story so far:

You have recently joined a group of pirates and are looking for the Lost Treaure of Bermuda. You found some treasureupon a dark rocky island. Taking the treasure onboard you head off back to sea towards the nearest harbor to redeem your riches. However, after a little while at sea disaster strikes. The creatures of the deep are out to get you and your crew. You suspect its a curse of the treasure you acquired, but escaping to shore is your only option. How long can you defend your ship before it is hopelessly overrun?
